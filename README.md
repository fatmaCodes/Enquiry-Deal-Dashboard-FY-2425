# 📊 Growth Tracker Dashboard — Enquiry & Deal Data FY 2024–25

> A multi-dimensional Excel dashboard engineered to analyze enquiry pipeline performance, deal conversion metrics, source attribution, geographic distribution, and employee contribution across FY 2024–25.

---

## 🔍 Project Overview

This dashboard was built to transform raw enquiry and deal data into actionable business intelligence. It consolidates over **39,840 total enquiries** into a single interactive interface — enabling stakeholders to drill down by time period, lead source, city, product, department, and individual contributor with zero manual filtering effort.

The project demonstrates end-to-end analytical thinking: from raw data structuring and pivot modeling to KPI design and visual storytelling.

## ✨ Dashboard Capabilities

### 🎛️ Interactive Controls
- **Year Slicer** — Toggle between FY 2024 and FY 2025 for year-over-year comparison
- **Month Slicer** — Isolate any month (Jan–Dec) for granular period analysis
- All visuals update dynamically in response to slicer selections

**Department-Wise Deal Performance**

## 💡 Analytical Insights Uncovered

- **IndM_Mail dominates** both deal and non-deal pipelines — the highest-volume acquisition channel and the most critical lever for conversion rate improvement
- **~3.4% conversion rate** across 39,840 enquiries signals significant pipeline leakage — a clear opportunity for lead qualification and nurturing process optimization
- **Mumbai and Bangalore** emerge as top enquiry cities, indicating geographic demand concentration worth targeting with dedicated sales efforts
- **Business Development** outperforms all departments with 411 closed deals — nearly 32% of total deal volume
- **Repeated Customers** contribute only 138 deals against 558 non-deals — pointing to an untapped customer retention and re-engagement opportunity

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download `Enquiry_Deal_Dashboard_FY2425.xlsx`
2. Open in **Microsoft Excel 2016 or later**
3. Navigate to the **Dashboard** sheet
4. Use the **Year** and **Month** slicers to filter the entire view
5. All charts and KPI cards update automatically based on selection
